What To Do

Adventure at Every Turn

Led by expert local guides who bring the land to life with stories, knowledge and genuine passion for the Mutoko wilderness.

Bush Walks

Track animals, learn bush medicine and discover ancient rock art with expert rangers.

village Drives

Sunrise and sunset drives in open 4×4s — spot antelope, baboons and dramatic golden light.

Our Commitment

Sustainability

Home / Sustainable
How We Operate

Luxury That Leaves the Land Better

We believe true luxury and environmental responsibility are inseparable. Every decision considers the land, the community and the next generation.

☀️

Solar Power

100% of the resort's energy is supplied by our solar farm with battery storage. Fully off-grid since 2019.

💧

Water Harvesting

Rainwater harvesting and closed-loop greywater recycling means we use 70% less water than comparable properties.

🌱

Organic Gardens

Our kitchen garden produces over 60% of guest vegetables — grown without pesticides using traditional Shona composting.

🐘

Wildlife Corridor

We partner with neighbouring landowners to maintain a 2,000-hectare corridor connecting the resort to Mutoko communal lands.

👥

Community Employment

95% of our staff come from within 15 km. We pay above-market rates and fund school scholarships for staff children.

♻️

Zero Waste

All organic waste is composted on-site. Plastics eliminated from guest operations. Zero-landfill status achieved 2022.

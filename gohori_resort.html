Our Story

A Sanctuary of
Refined Escape

Gohori Resort is Mutoko's finest destination for rest, recreation and rejuvenation. Set against a dramatic rocky hillside with lush gardens and serene courtyards, we offer an unforgettable blend of natural beauty and world-class comfort.

From our award-winning kitchen to the tranquil spa, every corner of Gohori has been crafted to inspire, restore and delight.
